AICS — Agenzia Italiana per la Cooperazione allo Sviluppo

AICS publishes evaluation, monitoring and consultancy tenders for Italian-funded development cooperation projects across Africa (Somalia, Ethiopia, Kenya, DRC, Sudan). Opportunities are issued via their local offices in Nairobi, Addis Ababa, Mogadishu, and Khartoum, published in the Italian Official Gazette (Gazzetta Ufficiale) and via TED for EU-co-funded projects. Their thematic calls (bandi tematici) target NGOs and civil society. No structured public tender portal is available; monitor their news and TED publications.
